Bases

Our carpentry offers you various models of bed bases constructed from high-quality, multi-layered evaporated beech, and Swiss stone pine. The curved slats, available in various sizes and flexibility, will provide you with a high level of ergonomics, which, when combined with the right mattress, will enhance your sleep quality.

Multipla

Information and Specifications
+
Our four-adjustment bed frame

The Multipla is a four-adjustment multi-layered beech wood bed base designed for latex and Foam-System mattresses, known for its enhanced anatomical features and body support.

– Technical specifications: 50x30 mm multi-layered beech wood frame, 14 multi-layered beech wood slats, 68 independent elastic rubber oscillating supports with varying rigidity, and 68 contoured elements in Swiss stone pine or multi-layered beech wood. Available in fixed or motorized versions.

Flexa

Information and Specifications
+
Our bed frame with flexibility adjusters

Flexa is a multi-layered beech wood bed base with flexibility adjusters to suit your preference. Suitable for all types of mattresses, Flexa is less rigid than the Bioform model, thanks to its narrower slats, it can provide greater elasticity.

– Technical details: 60x30 mm multi-layered beech wood frame, 26 slats embedded in elastic rubber oscillating supports. Double reinforcement slats for the hip area. Available in fixed or motorized versions.

Flexa Plus

Information and Specifications
+
The most flexible of our bed frames

It is a multilayer beech wood bed base with flexibility regulators at one's discretion and shock absorbers with slats perforated for the shoulders. Suitable for all types of mattresses, Flexa Plus is less rigid than the Bioform model; thanks to narrower slats, it manages to ensure greater elasticity.

– Technical details: 60x30 mm frame, 24 slats embedded in elastic rubber oscillating supports. The central support section features triple slats and variable-position rigidity adjusters. It includes 4 dampeners for the shoulder area. Available in fixed, single, or dual adjustment versions.

Orto Plus

Information and Specifications
+
Our universal bed frame

Ortoplus is a multi-layered beech wood bed base, suitable for all types of mattresses.

– Technical details: 24 multi-layered slats embedded in elastic rubber oscillating supports, rigidity adjustment on all slats. The central support section features triple slats and self-locking rigidity adjusters. It includes 4 dampeners for the shoulder area. Available in fixed, single, or dual adjustment versions.
